h2. Ben Blackburne

I currently work at the "Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute":http://www.sanger.ac.uk/. Previously I have worked at the "National Institute for Medical Research":http://www.nimr.mrc.ac.uk/ and I did my PhD at "The University of Nottingham":http://comp.chem.nottingham.ac.uk/.

h2. Current Projects

*Rome* is a probabilitic method to determine structure variations in DNA from new sequencing data.

*"Modiphy":http://github.com/benb/modiphy* is a library in Scala for implementing phylogenetic models.

*"TLF":http://github.com/benb/tlf/* is a Trivial Logging Framework for Scala
